✦ About Me

Passionate Roblox Developer
& Game Architect

I'm an 18-year-old Roblox game developer with over 7 years of hands-on experience creating games, systems, and interactive experiences. I have successfully led projects that reached more than 3,000,000 total plays, demonstrating both technical skill and player-focused design.

I specialize in building scalable systems using Luau, applying OOP principles, modular architecture, and performance optimization to deliver polished and professional gameplay. My work ranges from UI/UX systems to complex game mechanics and backend logic.

In addition to development, I work as an independent Roblox Studio tutor, teaching students scripting, game design, and publishing. I have also led workshops aimed at inspiring the next generation of game developers.

My goal is to become a professional game engineer and expand my skills into broader areas of the game development industry.
